Banana Cake Pop

Banana Cake Pop is our brand new GraphQL IDE which works well with Hot Chocolate and any other GraphQL server.

Banana Cake Pop is still in preview so try it out and give us feedback via our slack channel.

Banana Cake Pop uses 2-phase introspection to tune the IDE to the GraphQL features that are supported by a GraphQL server.

With 2-phase introspection Banana Cake Pop does not limit you to a certain GraphQL spec version but checks for certain features like repeatable directives or subscription support.

Banana Cake Pop lets you work with large schemas without slowing you down.

macOS Catalina:

With macOS Catalina Apple has begun to be stricter regarding apps that are not available through the app store.

We are working to get Apple`s new app notarization integrated into our build process. For now you have to right-click on the app after you have installed it and then click on open in the context menu.

After that a message box will open where you have to click on open in order to run Banana Cake Pop.
